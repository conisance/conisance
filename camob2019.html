About the Event

Conisance Access Management and Open Banking Summit is a path breaking networking forum, gathering key decision makers from the world's leading Organizations, Startups, and Governments. With thought provoking Case Studies, Keynotes and Panel Discussions, this summit will address the forthcoming challenges and derive best practices on subjects like Digital Identity, Digital Onboarding, and Open Banking.

Race to Bank the Under/ Unbanked

Realizing the potential of robust Digital Identification Systems will help every individual on this planet to gain access to their rights and services. Explore the role of Banks and Financial Institutions in building a global standard for Digital Identity by leveraging emerging technologies, as the inclusion frontier represents a massive $380 Billion opportunity for Global Business and Economic Development.

'Always be Onboarding' Mantra

The overwhelming effect of GAFA (Google, Amazon, Facebook, Apple) have changed the customer landscape. Embracing the rapid developments in technologies like AI, Big Data, Biometrics, Blockchain, etc. has helped financial institutions transform their legacy systems, but has it really made the customer experience frictionless? Get your hands on the optimal onboarding strategies that will play a critical role in meeting your business goals!

The Open 'X'perience

Open Banking as we know it may become outdated before it even gets off the ground as there is an opportunity to leapfrog into an integrated marketplace called Open X. Hence the focus will shift from Products to Customer Experience, Assets to Data, Shared Access instead of Ownership, and Collaborations. Experience the evolution around concepts like Lifestyle Banking, Hyper Personalization, Opti Channel experience and more!
