ACCESS MANAGEMENT & OPEN BANKING SUMMIT 2019

14th - 15th November 2019

London, UK.

About the Event

Conisance Access Management and Open Banking Summit is a path-breaking networking forum that will bring together key decision-makers from the world’s leading Organizations, Start-Ups, and Governments.

With thought-provoking Case Studies, Keynotes and Panel Discussions, this summit will address the forthcoming challenges and set new benchmarks on the best practices in Digital Identity, Digital Onboarding, and Open Banking.

Race to Bank the Unbanked

Discovering the true potential of the robust Digital Identification Systems will help many gain access to their rights and services. Explore the role of Banks and Financial Institutions in setting a global benchmark for Digital Identity by leveraging emerging technologies, as the inclusion frontier presents a massive $380 Billion opportunity for Global Business and Economic Development

'Always be Onboarding' Mantra

The overwhelming effect of ‘GAFA’ has changed the entire customer landscape. Embracing the rapid developments in technology like AI, Big Data, Biometrics, Blockchain, etc. has not only helped financial institutions transform their legacy, but also raised the standard of customer experience, forever. Get your hands on the optimal onboarding strategies that will play a critical role in meeting your business goals.

The Open
'X'perience

Open Banking, as we know it, may become outdated before it even takes off as there is an opportunity to leap into an integrated marketplace called Open X. Hence, the focus will shift from Products to Customer Experience, Assets to Data, Shared Access instead of Ownership along with Collaborations. Come, experience the evolution around concepts like Lifestyle Banking, Hyper-Personalization, Opti Channel experience and more!
