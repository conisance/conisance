Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for a conference?
You may register online by filling the registration form and duly completing the payment process. Once you register, you will receive an invoice on your email. Alternatively, you can download the Conisance App and register directly by filling the registration form. You can also download the registration form, fill it, and send us a scanned copy of the form through email. Our email id is support@conisance.com

Can I register for the conference by telephone?
No, filling the registration form is a mandate to register for the conference. Reach out to contact@conisance.com for details.

Will I receive a confirmation?
Yes, once you fill and submit the registration form, a confirmation will be sent to you on email.

What is included in my registration fee?
Your registration fee includes admission to the selected event, lunch, tea, and coffee. Please refer to the respective event agenda for more details. Registration fee also includes one year membership to the parent community of the respective event where you will get access to engage with all the other members.

Do I have to make my own hotel reservation?
Yes, you need to arrange for your own accommodation. Our venue partner may provide discounts that can be availed via a booking link for the registered delegates (subject to availability).

What is the conference dress attire?
You may choose between formal business attire or a business casual look.

Will the information/conference material provided at the conference be posted on the website?
Material provided at the conference will not be available anywhere else. However, conference presentations and keynotes will be made available to the participants subject to approval by the respective speakers.

Can I share my conference ticket with my colleagues?
Delegate tickets are not transferable throughout the conference duration unless this has been discussed with us and approval has been given for this prior to the event.

Are there options for my strict dietary requirements?
We try our best to make our delegates and members comfortable. Should you have any special requirements such as diet, mobility access, etc, you can let us know by sending us an email at support@conisance.com so that make prior arrangements.

What are the terms and conditions of participation?
Please refer the terms and conditions by clicking here.

Is there a way I can keep in touch with the people I meet?
Yes, absolutely. You will have access to our communities via the Conisance App and Website. You will be able to set up meetings, network, and engage with other attendees prior, during and after the conference. You can also scan the QR codes of attendees you meet at the conference and download a CSV of these contacts post-event so you can keep in touch with them.
